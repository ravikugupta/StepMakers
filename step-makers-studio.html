About the Studio

Where Passion
Meets the Beat

At Step Makers Dance & Fitness Studio, we believe movement is a language everyone speaks. With a team of dedicated, experienced instructors, we've created a space where beginners find confidence and seasoned dancers discover their next level.

Our classes span Bollywood, Hip-Hop, Contemporary, Freestyle, Folk Dance, and Zumba — delivered with multilingual choreography, high energy, and a community spirit you won't find anywhere else in BTM Layout.

Every session is designed to inspire, empower, and remind you that fitness can feel like a party.
